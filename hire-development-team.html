FAQ

What is an "extended team" as a service?

An extended team is a crew of skilled professionals that work as an extension of your in-house team. They integrate seamlessly into your company’s workflows and work with your internal teams to deliver projects. At Fourmeta, our extended teams feature skilled developers, designers, and project managers who align with your business goals and supplement your capabilities.

How is an extended team different from outsourcing?

Outsourcing often involves delegating entire projects to a third-party provider, while an extended team works directly with your internal team on specific tasks or projects. Fourmeta’s extended teams operate under your control and follow your processes.

What roles can an extended team fill?

At Fourmeta, our extended teams can provide a range of roles, including web and mobile developers, UI/UX designers, DevOps engineers, project managers, and QA specialists.

How does the onboarding process work with Fourmeta’s extended teams?

We begin by understanding your business goals, project requirements, and team dynamics. Then, we match you with our experts who align with your needs. We guide your new team members through an in-depth onboarding, familiarizing them with your workflows, tools, and communication methods.

Can I scale my extended team up or down based on project demands?

Yes, flexibility is one of the key benefits of using Fourmeta’s extended team services. You can scale your team up or down depending on the scope of your project, ensuring you only pay for the resources you need at any given time.

How does communication work with an extended team?

Fourmeta ensures that your dedicated team is fully integrated into your communication channels, whether it’s Slack, Microsoft Teams, or another platform. We also assign a project manager to give regular updates, progress reports, and daily meetings, ensuring transparent communication.

How do you ensure the quality of work from an extended team?

At Fourmeta, quality assurance is built into our process. Each team member goes through rigorous vetting and testing before being placed on a project. We also implement regular code reviews, testing, and feedback loops to ensure the highest standard of work.

What industries does Fourmeta cater to with extended teams?

Fourmeta’s extended teams have experience across a wide range of industries, including eCommerce, FinTech, healthcare, education, and SaaS. Our flexible model allows us to adapt to industry-specific needs.

How much control do I have over my extended team?

You have full control over your extended team. While Fourmeta handles administrative tasks like hiring, onboarding, and HR, you maintain control over project management, daily tasks, and how your team operates within your organization.

What is the pricing model for an extended team service?

At Fourmeta, pricing is based on a monthly retainer for each team member or a customized project-based model depending on your requirements. We provide transparent pricing, allowing you to plan your budget accordingly while still accessing top-tier talent.

How quickly can Fourmeta deploy an extended team?

We pride ourselves on quickly assembling teams to meet your needs. Depending on the required skill sets, Fourmeta can deploy an extended team within a few weeks, ensuring minimal downtime and immediate project progression.

Is there a minimum commitment period for using an extended team?

We understand that business needs can change, so we offer flexible commitment periods. While most clients engage with our extended teams for several months, we can accommodate shorter or longer engagements based on your project’s scope.

Will the extended team work in the same time zone as my company?

Fourmeta offers flexible working hours to accommodate your preferred time zone. We ensure overlap for critical meetings and updates to keep projects running smoothly. Our teams can work in various time zones, including European, North American, and Asia-Pacific time zones.

How do I monitor the performance of my extended team?

We provide regular performance reports and use key metrics to measure progress. Fourmeta’s project management tools allow for real-time tracking of deliverables and milestones, ensuring that your extended team is meeting expectations and timelines.

Can I have a dedicated project manager for my extended team?

Yes, Fourmeta assigns a dedicated project manager to every extended team engagement. The project manager ensures clear communication between you and your extended team, manages timelines and oversees the overall project execution.
